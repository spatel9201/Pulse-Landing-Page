How It Works

Set it up once. Get insights every morning.

01

Sign up and get your Pulse email

Create your account, add your properties, and we'll assign you a unique Pulse email address. We'll walk you through setting up CSV report scheduling — takes about 10 minutes per property.

02

Forward your night audit reports

Add your Pulse email to your brand's night audit report schedule. Once reports start arriving, Pulse automatically parses, normalizes, and contextualizes your data across every property.

03

That's it. Insights come to you.

Every morning you'll get a daily email with your portfolio's vital signs — before your first cup of coffee. Click through to the full dashboard anytime you want to dig deeper. No daily work required.
